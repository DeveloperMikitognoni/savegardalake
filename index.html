#SAVEGARDALAKE

Plastic is harmful to the lake, use recycle bins!

What is SeaBin?

Seabin is a project started in Australia aimed to supply all over the world the Seabin technology, which is a device that, through a pump and a basket made of a thin net, is capable of cleaning the water from the plastic pollution.
The device installed at the Fraglia della vela has more of a research goal rather than cleaning the lake, and has these characteristics:.

  • Pump of 25.000 liters per hour
  • Ability of absorbing a kg and a half of waste every day
  • Picks up fragments with a length up to 2 mm

What can you do?

Do not leave things on the beach!

All the waste you leave on the beaches ends up in the lake, polluting it.

Use recycle bins!

If everyone uses recycling bins, plastic pollution will be extremely limited.

Manholes go into the lake!

Manholes throw the water passing through them directly in the lake without any filter, so all the waste you throw there reaches the lake.
